1 Author's Introduction

Author Li Xinye is described as a graduate of Tsinghua University's Yao Class, MIT, and Brown University, and founder of Alpha Star Research. The book was first published in October 2025 in New York. The opening chapter uses the author's personal emotional journey spanning over a decade with Guo Juyang as both narrative thread and case study for the book.

"This handbook written by a world-class AI engineer also has a touch of literary flavor."

2 Narrative Structure

The first chapter presents a timeline through multiple photographs: from filming Guo Juyang in high school, to her graduation certificate, social media posts flaunting a wealthy spouse, and the transformation of her attitude after learning of the author's economic resources. These visual materials serve as empirical foundations for the theoretical analyses in later chapters.

4 Writing Style & Methodology

Chapter 1 establishes the book's literary style: interweaving personal narrative with theoretical analysis, using personal emotional experiences as a case-study database. The author indicates that subsequent chapters will progressively unfold economic analysis, legal analysis, moral analysis, attraction principles, and practical guides on dating married women.
