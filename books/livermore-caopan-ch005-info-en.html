Livermore's Trading Method · Ch.5 "The Pivotal Point"

SCQA · Situation → Conflict → Question → Answer"I never benefited much from a move if I did not get in at somewhere near the beginning of that move." Through over 30 years of painstaking research, Livermore fused time and price into the "Pivotal Point" method. Markets always move in trends, and every major move has a critical breakout level — 50, 100, 200, 300 are natural integer pivotal points. Most traders lack patience, entering too early or too late. Answer: wait at pivotal levels, confirm direction with the "3-point breakthrough" rule, build positions progressively, watch the market unfold, and exit decisively at danger signals.

This chapter represents the pinnacle of Livermore's trading system. Through over 30 years of iterative refinement, he improved his recording method again and again until, by fusing the time element with price movement, his records finally began to speak to him. "From then on … I have rarely been left out of the big moves when I had a pivotal point to guide me." Through three classic battles — Anaconda Copper (100→300), Bethlehem Steel (87¾→155), and Cocoa futures (600-point rally) — he proved the method's power. He even shares an amusing anecdote: a lady pestered him for a tip at a party; he recommended Cerro de Pasco (which had just broken its pivotal point). The stock rose 15 points in a week — but she never bought it, because she "wanted to see if his tip was accurate first."

01
Core Concept
What Are Pivotal Points — The Critical Psychological Moment When a Trend Launches
Pivotal points are the critical psychological price levels where trends launch — especially integer levels like 50, 100, 200, and 300. Once effectively breached, they almost invariably trigger straight-line rapid movements. Livermore calls these "the critical psychological moment marking the beginning of a move." Trade at pivotal points, and you never have to worry about losses.

02
Signal Recognition
Dual Signals: Breakthrough Confirmed & Failed — The Market Gives Both
Using the concrete example of $40→$45→$49½, Livermore reveals two classic trading signals around pivotal points — the market gives both positive entry signals and negative exit signals, provided you have the patience to wait.
Failed to Break Pivotal Point
→ positive signal →
Price rallies 3 points from the reaction low → buy immediately. Market's refusal to break signals selling exhaustion, birth of new trend
Broke Pivotal Point but Under 3 Pts
→ positive signal →
Dipped below $40 but by less than 3 pts, then rallied to $43 (pivotal + 3) → also buy. Inability to sustain the break confirms uptrend
▼ Core Rule ▼
Validating the Breakthrough
→ condition →
If the market validates the new trend decisively, price will continue past $49½ — and exceed it by 3 points or more

05
Trading Discipline
The Trader's Path: Patience & Discipline Are the Core of the Pivotal Point Method
Livermore confesses: "Every time I lost patience and traded recklessly instead of waiting for the pivotal point, I always lost money." You might profit through tips or others' guidance — but you will find that independently earned profits bring far greater satisfaction.
✅

The Right Way

Wait patiently for pivotal points, discover opportunities independently, watch closely for danger signals, maintain records by hand, profit through your own judgment

❌

The Wrong Way

Trade recklessly without patience, rely on tips or others' advice, ignore danger signals, neglect price record study, depend on others for guidance

🔑 Key Takeaway
The essence of the Pivotal Point Method: the market will, at the proper time, give you both positive entry signals and negative exit signals — if only you have the patience to wait. By combining the time element with price records and maintaining them by hand, you will discover pivotal points and use them to build positions. When a stock lacks follow-through after crossing a pivotal point, the market easily reverses — close your position immediately. Studying pivotal points is like mining gold — incredibly fascinating, and the profits you earn independently will bring unmatched satisfaction.
