ENTERPRISE CASE
Lenovo's FuPan Methodology
FuPan is Lenovo's core methodology, playing a decisive role in every major event. Liu Chuanzhi promoted FuPan at Lenovo from 2001, elevating it to one of the three pillars of the "Lenovo Methodology" — "growing through FuPan." Facing fierce competition from Dell, Lenovo's FuPan revealed that the real issue was not direct-sales vs. distribution, but process efficiency and customer reach. This insight led to a decisive turnaround in 2004. The 2009 repurchase of its mobile business likewise stemmed from deep FuPan of the industry and Apple's strategy.

HISTORICAL WISDOM
Zeng Guofan's FuPan Wisdom
Zeng Guofan's transformation from rigid directness to soft resilience is a classic example of deep FuPan. A man of modest talent, Zeng became the "First Man of the Qing Restoration" through his powerful habit of retrospective reflection. During his year-long mourning leave, he analyzed the root of his conflicts with the bureaucratic establishment — his direct, forceful approach, though satisfying, planted hidden dangers. Upon his return, he visited every yamen in Changsha with humility, winning hearts and building a solid rear for his campaigns, ultimately achieving the Confucian ideal of "immortality in virtue, words, and deeds."

EVERYDAY PRACTICE
An Ordinary Person's FuPan Practice
FuPan is not reserved for great figures — even a minor fainting episode can yield profound insights through careful review. Ajun overate during the Mid-Autumn Festival, causing stomach distress. Standing up too quickly triggered transient hypotension, leading to fainting and a head collision with a wall corner. Through FuPan, he traced the physiological chain of chest tightness → dizziness → vomiting, and grasped a deeper truth: "Balance is the optimal working environment for all body parts" — illness means balance is disrupted, not that the body is inherently weak.

METHOD GUIDE
When to FuPan
Any time, any place, any event — whenever necessary, you can conduct a FuPan. Lenovo's decade of practice distilled three principles: review small matters promptly, review major matters at milestones, and conduct comprehensive reviews after completion. FuPan your performance after meetings, your strategy after negotiations, the causes of success, and the lessons of failure. The key is making FuPan a habit and instinct — reaching the state of "awakening": awareness, enlightenment, and perception.
