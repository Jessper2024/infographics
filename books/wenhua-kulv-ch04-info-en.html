02
Legendary Figure
Li Bing: From Political Plan to Ecological Plan
An imperial appointment for Qin's unification campaign became, in Li Bing's hands, an ecological mission—his task was not governing people but governing water. He codified 'dredge deep, build dams low' and 'cut angles at bends,' used as hydraulic canon until the 20th century.

🔑 Key Takeaway
Dujiangyan embodies Chinese civilization's clearest administrative principle—Li Bing transformed a political plan into an ecological one, replacing 'conquering nature' with 'understanding and following water.' Two millennia later, the water still flows by his design. This legacy teaches us: true civilization lies not in monumental construction but in continuously nourishing the daily lives of ordinary people.
