WHY
Why Fupan Became a Hot Keyword
The Fupan wave is no accident — it is an inevitable choice in the VUCA era. Four underlying reasons drive its popularity:
1
VUCA environment driving change: As conditions grow volatile, uncertain, complex, and ambiguous, individuals and organizations need faster, more effective learning. Fupan, as a structured experiential learning method, is easy to learn and quick to apply.
2
No precedents in innovation domains: In areas of disruptive innovation, there are no mature experiences to directly "borrow." You must "cross the river by feeling the stones," using Fupan for rapid iteration and knowledge creation.
3
The era demands deep introspection: As society grows increasingly restless, more people seek stillness and inner depth. Fupan as a method of self-reflection and deep inquiry grows ever more valuable.
4
Rapid organizational knowledge extraction: Fupan is a method for quickly extracting organizational experience and sharing wisdom in the mobile internet era — turning results into cases, micro-courses, and knowledge base assets.
Ecological principle: For an organism to survive and develop, its learning speed (L) must be ≥ the speed of environmental change (C) — L ≥ C. Fupan is the core tool for enhancing organizational learning capability.

COST
Not Doing Fupan = Paying Tuition Twice
If you or your organization exhibit any of the following four patterns, you need to learn and apply Fupan. In daily work, we often see:
1
"Low-level" repetition: New hires lack experience causing service decline; veterans sigh at years of ups and downs — no experience transfer mechanism exists.
2
Repeating the same mistakes: A salesperson gets rejected and regrets, "Why did I make the same mistake again?" — the personal learning loop is broken.
3
Safety incidents recurring: A gas leak at the Cangzhou factory, and a near-identical incident at the Kunshan factory just last month — "Why doesn't anyone remember?" — organizational memory is missing.
4
Knowledge stranded: Information is at everyone's fingertips via a smartphone, yet many merely Ctrl-C+Ctrl-V without analysis, verification, or synthesis — real capability never grows.
Fupan is a miraculous method — Zeng Guofan was forged by it. Liu Chuanzhi championed it at Lenovo. Wang Jianlin built it into Wanda's mechanisms. The US Army uses it to cultivate leadership. BP saved $700M in 2007 alone through Fupan. At Huawei's strategic reserve corps, Fupan achieved 100% coverage.
