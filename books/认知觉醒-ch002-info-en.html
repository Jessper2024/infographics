Life's Essence
Fuzziness is the Root of Trouble: Life is a Race to Eliminate It
Consciousness stratification brought huge benefits but also a side effect — fuzziness. The subconscious easily influences consciousness, but consciousness struggles to access the subconscious. People constantly do things they don't understand: wanting to study but reaching for the phone instead. Fuzziness breeds confusion and fear, which then shape life's trajectory. Those with less fuzziness live clearer; those with more live in chaos. Life's competition is essentially about the speed and pattern of eliminating fuzziness.

Supreme Ability
Intuition Over Reason: The Astonishing Power of Subconscious
Subconscious processes at 11,000,000 ops/s; consciousness at only 40 ops/s — a 275,000× gap. Churchill changed car doors on instinct and dodged a bomb; Lincoln rejected a cabinet member on instinct. Both captured subconscious signals their conscious minds missed. 'Going by feeling' is the top method because it helps us sense what truly suits us, placing us in the stretch zone. Intuition selects first, reason analyzes second — that's the expert's learning strategy.

03
Learning Method
Circuit-Breaker Reading: Don't Greed, Just Capture Sparks
Cheng Jia's 'Circuit-Breaker Reading': when you encounter something inspiring, stop immediately. Then ask three questions: ① Why did this point inspire me? ② Can I apply this insight in 3 different contexts? ③ What similar knowledge connects to this? Being well-read is less important than reading well. Take just ONE most inspiring point from a book, practice and change — the yield far exceeds finishing the book while staying at the 'knowing' level.

04
Cognitive Evolution
Eliminating Cognitive Fuzziness: The True Purpose of Learning
The purpose of learning is to eliminate fuzziness, and the method to acquire knowledge is also to eliminate fuzziness — purpose and method are unified. Humans naturally dislike learning and thinking because they consume energy. Yet elites in every field simply know more and see more clearly. Top students' secret is their error notebook — they invest time clarifying mistakes and attacking them. Average students repeat what they already know, ignoring real difficulties. The difference isn't diligence but the pattern of effort: who's more willing to do high-energy tasks — eliminating fuzziness.

05
Action Breakthrough
Eliminate Emotional & Action Fuzziness: Face It, Break It Down
The real cause of weak action is choice fuzziness. When we lack sufficiently clear instructions or goals, we instinctively choose pleasure over mentally demanding options. Solution: break goals and processes into concrete, specific steps — create a single-track path from many possibilities, leaving yourself 'no choice.' Emotional fuzziness is equally fatal — avoiding pain doesn't make it disappear; it pushes it into the subconscious where boundaries expand infinitely. The only way: face it, see it clearly, break it down, dissolve it. Fear bullies the timid; face it and it reveals its true form.
