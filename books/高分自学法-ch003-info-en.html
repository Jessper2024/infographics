Self-Study for High Scores · Preface「Why I Wrote This Book」

SCQA · Situation → Complication → Question → Answer In 2017, the term "self-study" (独学) was not yet mainstream — the author aspired to write a "learning method bible" unswayed by trends. The pandemic exposed the truth: most students lack self-directed learning ability. Yet Newton's breakthroughs during the plague proved solitude is the best time for learning. How do you write a timeless "bible" of learning? The answer: root "self-study" (独学) as "soul-study" (魂学) — maximize solitary study time with correct methods.

BLUF: Self-study is not a passing trend — it is the essence of learning. The author's motivation was not to ride a wave, but to trim away the branches and preserve the roots: a "bible" that anyone can use to master learning methods. In English, "alone" means "all one" — when you choose solitude, you concentrate everything on one thing, transforming self-study into soul-study.

01
Learning Revolution
The Misunderstood Learning Revolution: "Self-Study"
At a time when "eating alone" and "drinking alone" gained social resonance before "studying alone," the author bucked the trend. In 2017, solitary eating and drinking had captured public imagination, but solitary learning was still far from becoming a buzzword. The author did not write because he foresaw a trend — his motivation was the opposite: to create a "bible of learning methods" rooted in fundamentals. After the book succeeded in 2018, Gwanghwamun's major bookstore established a "Self-Study Mode" section, bringing the concept into the mainstream.

02
Bible Vision
Trim the Branches, Leave a Learning Book for Generations
The author aimed not to write a bestseller, but a "learning method bible" to be passed down. By pruning unnecessary details and preserving only the roots and trunk — the fundamentals and core — anyone can master learning through this book. His choice to use the most distilled language to express the most fundamental truths paradoxically created depth and longevity.
"I hope you write a book that endures, one my child can still read when they grow up."
— The editor to the author
03
Core Insight
External Help Only Leads the Horse to Water
Cram schools, tutoring, and online courses are merely tools — real learning must be done alone. The biggest difference between top performers and everyone else is "maximizing solitary study time with correct methods." External aids are like leading a horse to water — but drinking is up to the horse. To excel, you must increase self-study time and use the right approach.
⚠️ Critical distinction: Good reference books, famous courses, and smart learning tools are not learning itself — they are only aids for stages 1 through 3. Learning must be done alone.

04
Pandemic Revelation
COVID Exposed the Truth: The Absence of Self-Directed Learning
The 2020 pandemic tore open a wound — most students' learning ability plummeted. The reason was clear: they didn't know how to self-study. Students who had been chained to desks by school and cram schools found their attention hijacked by games, webtoons, YouTube, and Netflix during long hours at home. Yet the author offers a counter-perspective: the pandemic created the ideal conditions for learning.

05
Philosophical Depth
Alone = All One: From Self-Study to Soul-Study
Studying alone is not isolation — it is integration. In English, "alone" means "all one" — everyone is one. When you choose solitude, you concentrate everything on a single pursuit. In Korean, "self-study" (独学) and "soul-study" (魂学) are homophones, hinting at a higher pursuit: transforming solitary study into deep learning infused with soul. Newton, while sheltering from the plague in 1665 at his countryside home, systematized calculus, studied color with prisms, and discovered gravity — all through the power of solitude.
"Though I wrote this book, it was you who completed it. Through your practice, you validated that this is an unchanging method of learning, one that can endure."
— The author to readers
🔑 Key Takeaway
Trends shift unexpectedly, but the essence of learning remains constant. The best learning method is "maximizing solitary study time with correct methods." Cram schools, tutoring, and online courses are only forces that lead the horse to water — drinking is always your own action. Believe you can learn well. Increase your self-study time. Transform "self-study" (独学) into "soul-study" (魂学) — this is where it all begins.
