Su Dongpo Biography · Ch.1 "Original Preface"

SCQA · Core Insight Why has every generation of Chinese for a thousand years sincerely admired Su Dongpo? Because he possessed a multifaceted genius rich in variety, change, and humor — outstanding intellect, yet a heart like an innocent child. Lin Yutang wrote this biography for no other reason than he simply wanted to: one soul paying tribute to another.

In his preface, Lin Yutang unveils the full panorama of Su Dongpo's multifaceted genius — an incurable optimist, a great humanitarian, a friend of the people, a great prose master, a great calligrapher, an innovative painter, a wine experimenter, an engineer, a yoga practitioner, a Buddhist, a Confucian statesman. Yet even this does not capture him fully. Su Dongpo's charm is like a woman's allure or a flower's fragrance — easy to sense, yet hard to analyze. "At the mention of Su Dongpo, the Chinese always smile with warmth and affection."

01
Motivation
Why Did Lin Yutang Write About Su Dongpo?
Lin Yutang said: he wrote this biography for no other reason than he wanted to — for years the idea had been in his mind. In 1936, going to America, he brought Su Dongpo's rare editions with him, hoping to "have him by my side" in a foreign land. This biography is not cold academic research, but one soul paying tribute to another.

02
Multifaceted Genius
The Incurable Optimist: Su Dongpo's Many Identities
Su Dongpo was an incurable optimist, a great humanitarian, a friend of the people, a great prose master, a great calligrapher, an innovative painter, a wine experimenter, an engineer, a yoga practitioner, a Buddhist, a Confucian statesman. But he was naturally unsuited to political cunning — his poetry and prose were natural outpourings of the heart.

03
Personal Magnetism
Why Is Su Dongpo's Charm So Hard to Describe?
Su Dongpo's charm is like a woman's allure or a flower's fragrance — easy to sense, yet hard to analyze. He wielded his brush like a toy, and from its tip came music reflecting every human emotion. He joked freely with friends and enemies alike, could be angry at things, but was incapable of hating people.

04
Philosophy of Life
Three Philosophies Fused: The Secret of the Joyful Genius
Su Dongpo was a Buddhist in metaphysics, knowing life is the temporary manifestation of an eternal soul; but he could not accept that life is a burden — he himself savored every moment. From Buddhism, Confucianism, and Taoism, his mind forged a new alloy.

05
Poet's True Nature
A Story of the Soul: Natural and Faithful
Su Dongpo's story is fundamentally the story of a soul — he had no schemes, no goals; he sang, wrote, and commented along the way, simply wanting to express what was in his heart, regardless of consequences. His works radiate a vivid personality — always sincere, earnest, and never self-deceiving.

🔑 Key Takeaway
Su Dongpo's charm lies in his absolute fidelity to himself — he cannot be defined by labels; he is a spiritual specimen proving the possibility of "living authentically." As he himself said: "Above, I can accompany the Jade Emperor; below, I can keep company with beggars. Before my eyes, there is not a single bad person in the world."
