FuPan · Chapter 24「Know Yourself, Elevate Your Character」

SCQA · Situation → Conflict → Question → Answer Beyond external value, FuPan elevates character — for both individuals and organizations. Yet most swell with pride in success and despair in failure, never asking why. How can FuPan help you see your true measure and achieve equanimity in victory and defeat? Persistent reflection — as Vantone 30-year Reflection Day demonstrates — transforms temperament.

FuPan distinguishes root causes from chance — mastery or luck? Thinking error or execution gap? More review deepens reflection, steadies temperament. Feng Lun institutionalized Reflection Day at Vantone, every September 13 since 1992 — a 30-year tradition of systematic self-examination covering strategy to execution.
