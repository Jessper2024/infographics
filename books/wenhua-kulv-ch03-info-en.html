🔑 Key Takeaway
Mountains and rivers are the ultimate benchmark of all culture. China's three heaven-earth lines (Yellow River, Yangtze, Great Wall) determined its civilizational DNA, and climate has wielded more power over millennia than any dynastic change. Through twenty years of walking, Yu Qiuyu demonstrates that returning from text to ecology is the truest form of cultural self-awareness.
