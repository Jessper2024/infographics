02
Behavioral
Five Fatal Trading Mistakes
❌ Averaging Down
Added to losing shorts at every new high, compounding risk
✅ Never Add to Losers
Stop out or hold still. Never double down
❌ No Stop Loss
Relied entirely on gut feel, zero hard stop protection
✅ Hard Stop Always
Set stop loss on entry. Protect capital first
❌ Direction Chaos
Went long and short simultaneously, lost on both sides
✅ One Direction Only
Long or short — pick one. Switch only after deliberate review
❌ Maxed Out Position
Full margin, maximum risk exposure
✅ Position Sizing
Single trade risk capped at 1–2% of capital
❌ TP Discipline Collapse
Kept raising take-profit targets until profit became loss, then liquidation
✅ Staged Take-Profit
Scale out at preset levels. Don't let greed move the goalposts

05
Resolution
Four Iron Rules: Non-Negotiable Going Forward
❶
One Direction Principle
Only one directional bet at a time. Any switch requires a thorough review first.
❷
Four-Price System
Entry → Stop Loss → Breakeven → Take Profit. All four mandatory. Protect capital before chasing profit.
❸
Staged Take-Profit Discipline
Scale out at preset levels. Never continuously raise profit targets.
❹
Never Add to a Losing Position
Two options only when underwater: stop out, or hold as-is. Never double the bet.
