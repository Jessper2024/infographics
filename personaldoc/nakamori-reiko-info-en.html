Her Secret Weapon: The "Heavy Sweating" Phenomenon

In a market where the "sweat fetish" genre commands fierce demand, this became her deadliest asset — other actresses rely on heavy makeup that melts under sweat; her bare face withstood the test, getting better as the scene intensified.
