Pinduoduo: Organization & Culture Restructuring

Q1 2026 Earnings Call · Zhao Jiazhen & Chen Lei · "3-Year Reinvention" in Full Swing
Pinduoduo Q1 2026 revenue reached RMB 106.2 billion (+11% YoY), while net profit fell to RMB 12.5 billion (-15% YoY). Co-CEO Zhao Jiazhen declared the company has entered its "second decade," launching a comprehensive organizational and cultural restructuring centered on the "3-Year Reinvention" strategy. A dedicated company was established in Shanghai with an initial RMB 15 billion injection — marking Pinduoduo's leap from pure marketplace to a platform + owned-brand ecosystem.
01
Q1 Results
Revenue Tops RMB 100B, Profit Down — by Design
Revenue grew to RMB 106.2B (+11% YoY), yet net profit dropped 15%. This is not deterioration — it's strategic profit redeployment. Pinduoduo is consciously reinvesting earnings into supply chain and brand development. Management was explicit: "We prioritize long-term value from ecosystem reinvestment and supply chain commitment over short-term results." The profit decline is proof that the RMB 100-billion support plan and 3-Year Reinvention are real, funded bets.

02
Strategy Shift
3-Year Reinvention: From Marketplace to Brand Owner
Pinduoduo's boldest strategic pivot yet: moving from a pure platform model into first-party brand operations. A dedicated Shanghai company was established in March 2025, with an initial RMB 15B cash injection and a 3-year commitment of RMB 100B. Teams are embedding in industrial clusters, co-creating with global IP partners, and incubating owned brands across multiple categories and markets. This is not tactical — it's genetic-level organizational transformation.

03
Model Innovation
The Platform Absorbs Risk — Supply Chains Gain Certainty
The core logic: internalize manufacturing risk as platform responsibility. In the traditional model, factories alone bear all the uncertainty and trial-and-error costs of brand development. Pinduoduo's owned-brand model delivers sales certainty directly to the supply chain — knowing how much will sell, at what price, and to whom gives factories the confidence to invest in R&D and process innovation. The result is a multi-win positive-sum cycle.

04
Ecosystem Investment
From Farmland to Factory Belt — RMB 100B at Every Link
The RMB 100-billion support plan is precision-targeted across three fronts: agricultural depth, industrial-belt enablement, and logistics infrastructure. First-wave DodoGoods projects span oranges, pineapples, matcha, goji berries, and seafood regions. The New-Quality Supply initiative reaches snack, jewelry, and lighting industrial clusters. The "E-commerce Goes West" program has slashed remote-area logistics costs by nearly 80%.

Org Restructure
Hardship Is the Engine of Self-Revolution
As Zhao Jiazhen put it: "Early difficulties are inevitable — but they are precisely what drive a team's self-revolution." In Q1 alone, PDD rolled out 20+ food-safety governance measures, with violator store processing now measured in hours. Food safety compliance, social responsibility, organizational management, and cultural transformation are not separate projects — they are three facets of one unified restructuring.
