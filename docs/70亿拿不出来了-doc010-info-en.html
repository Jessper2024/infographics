03Musk's Space Revolution – $100/kg to Orbit

Musk announced Starship's full reuse will bring launch costs to $100/kg — 2.5% of Falcon's current $3,000-4,000/kg.

Two factors: Starship payload is 8-9× Falcon's; Falcon only reuses the lower stage (upper stage costs $10M and is discarded), while Starship is designed for full reuse. Only at this cost level does space solar power and space compute become economically viable.

04Silver's Brutal Rally – $28 to $103 in 9 Months

Silver broke through $100 to hit $103+, surging from $28 in just 9 months. Nearly every short was crushed.

The author advises against setting target prices for such trends — once you have a target, you'll be tempted to short when it's exceeded. "Gold is at 4990, institutions keep raising targets to 5500 — like dangling a carrot in front of a donkey."

"Whatever can be calculated and valued belongs to a bear market. This is a bull market — price by sentiment first."

05Survival Rules – Dividend Traps & Sentiment Pricing

Three lessons: ① High-dividend promises are meaningless when profits collapse; ② In a bull market, price by sentiment, not fundamentals — calculating valuation causes early exit; ③ Never set price targets on extreme trends — silver shorts learned this the hard way.
