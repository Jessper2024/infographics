Looking back five years, Wang Xing acknowledged that Community Group Buy was a major strategic mistake and has decisively curtailed losses. On AI, Meituan refuses to blindly follow the hype, instead focusing on landing AI in food delivery and retail's core scenarios. Meanwhile, the social costs of the "everything to your door" model — rider welfare, traffic pressure, etc. — have sparked widespread public debate.

📌 Key Takeaway

Meituan is at a strategic inflection point — shifting from "growth first" to "efficiency first." In the short term, RMB 65 billion in monetizable assets and buyback plans provide a valuation floor. In the medium term, the food delivery competitive landscape is stabilizing, and the instant retail dual-engine strategy is beginning to bear fruit. In the long run, the pace of overseas expansion and the discipline around AI investment will determine whether Meituan evolves from "China's largest local life platform" into a "global instant retail giant." Wang Xing answered market anxiety with candor, but ultimately, results must speak for themselves.
