Always Hesitant in Trading?
Here's How Real Confidence Is Built

XTradingTime Trading Training · 2026/06/26 · mp.weixin.qq.com

SCQA: Traders universally face hesitation and lack of confidence — afraid to enter, unable to hold profits, constantly changing systems, and relying on others' opinions. Most people try to fix this as a personality flaw with slogans and motivation, but that's the wrong direction. Where does real trading confidence come from? The answer: confidence is a byproduct of evidence, built on two cornerstones — positive expectancy validation + discipline execution proof.
