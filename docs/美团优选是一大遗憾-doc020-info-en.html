Meituan's Wang Xing: Meituan Select Was a Major Regret

Business Observer · 2026/06/27 · Source: mp.weixin.qq.com

SCQA Overview: Meituan held its annual shareholder meeting where Wang Xing and CFO Chen Shaohui reviewed the past five years. Wang Xing openly admitted two major regrets — entering overseas markets too late and Meituan Select failing to meet expectations after five years of investment. Why did Meituan Select fail? Where is Meituan heading? The answer lies in the transition from asset-light grocery retail with no consumer loyalty to H快乐猴's supply-chain-driven extreme value model.

📝 Key Takeaway

The failure of Meituan Select reveals a profound lesson: asset-light grocery e-commerce generates zero consumer loyalty, and pure price competition inevitably spirals below sustainability. Wang Xing's candid reflection and Happy Monkey's supply-chain pivot signal a new era for community retail — one built on deep supply chain integration and private brand development. With ¥65 billion in realizable assets, restarted buybacks, and a founder who hasn't sold a single share, Meituan is positioning for long-term value creation.
