04CATL Lithium & Auto Exports

CATL's Jianxiawo lithium mine retains mining rights; land-use permit expired due to new regulations, mining halted since August last year. Currently re-filing permits, 1+ year minimum to resume. Reduced supply, sentiment rebound, lithium rallied.

Jan-May auto exports 4.059M units +63%, May alone 930K +68%, NEVs 45%. But Europe has begun targeting China's capacity — future exports uncertain. Domestic auto sales weak, capacity rising, localized overcapacity emerging.
